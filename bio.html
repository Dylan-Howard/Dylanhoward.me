Dylan Howard
Born in Bowling Green, Kentucky, I've been fortunate to live in what I consider the most beautiful place on earth. This natural beauty of my home encouraged me to begin landscape photography. I've had the oppertunity to travel throughout Africa. As I matured as a person and photographer, I discovered that the true beauty of a location is it's people. I fell in love with portrait photography because of the wide array of emotions people can display and the uniqueness of each person.
"The world is a book, and those who do not travel read only a page." - St. Augustine
